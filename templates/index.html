Welcome to AccessiBridge

AccessiBridge is designed to break down communication and accessibility barriers for people who are blind, deaf, or deafblind. Our tools help you navigate the digital world with ease.

For Everyone

For Deafblind Users

Our tools are designed with deafblind users in mind. Features include:

  • Simplified text output - Clear, structured descriptions optimized for braille displays
  • High contrast design - Maximum visual clarity for those with partial vision
  • Keyboard navigation - Full keyboard accessibility for all features
  • Haptic feedback - Vibration alerts when supported by your device
  • Text simplification - AI-powered text simplification for easier understanding

Accessibility Features

  • Full screen reader compatibility (NVDA, JAWS, VoiceOver)
  • High contrast mode support
  • Keyboard-only navigation
  • Large click/touch targets
  • Clear, readable fonts at large sizes
  • Reduced motion support
  • ARIA labels and roles throughout
